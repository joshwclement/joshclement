The tvOS GUI Kit

My second idea for Big Screen UI, was to create a free Sketch template that makes it easy to create beautiful apps for Apple TV.

Check out the tvOS GUI Kit. There's been thousands of downloads, shares, likes and recommendations. It's been featured in Sketch Resources, Product Hunt and even impressed Apple UI Kit senior software engineer Marshall Huss!

The kit includes grid layouts, 14 predefined layout templates and 13 recreated system apps.

Every container, link and icon was pixel-perfect, customizable and ready to re-use for any TV project.
