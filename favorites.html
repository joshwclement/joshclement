Initial customer feedback has been positive. "Thankyou." "Wonderful". It’s too early to tell. Data to come. But we’ll look for certain types of behavior. are a lot of people using it? why? or not? Any change in major metrics (cancels, retention) Number of favorites - playback style stuff going on? Favoriting workouts they’ve played before, or they’ve never done before (queuing vs bookmarking use cases) Do people use the library/search less? How does it compare with active users.
