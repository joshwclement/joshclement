Handpicked UI patterns

The first project for Big Screen, was simply collecting screenshots of apps that I liked. UI patterns abound for other platforms, like iOS or Android, but not so much for TV. There's now a huge resource of hundreds of high quality screenshots, covering tvOS, Android TV and even Roku. I even wrote a how-to screenshot guide and opened up the site to public submissions. Many hands make light work.

The tvOS Kit

My second project for Big Screen UI, was a free Sketch template that makes it easy to create beautfiul apps for Apple TV. I referenced Apple's tvOS Human Interface Guidelines, and created a document as accurate as possible. The kit included grid layouts, 14 predefined layout templates and 13 recreated system apps. Most importantly (for designers) every container, link and icon was pixel-perfect, customizable and ready to re-use for any TV project.

The tvOS Kit was clearly something designers were looking for. It has seen thousands of downloads, shares, likes and recommendations. Featured in Sketch Resources, Product Hunt and impressed Apple UI Kit senior software engineer Marshall Huss!
